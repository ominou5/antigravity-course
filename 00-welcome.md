# Welcome to Getting Started With Antigravity

---

## What Is This Course?

This course teaches you how to use **Antigravity** — an AI coding IDE — to build real software. By the end, you'll have:

- A live web app with a database, accessible from any browser
- A real URL you can share with anyone
- The skills to build and ship more apps on your own

You don't need any coding experience to start. You don't need to know how websites work. You don't need to memorize anything.

What you do need: Curiosity, a computer, and a willingness to try things.

---

## The Philosophy of This Course

> **Using Antigravity to Learn Antigravity.**

Every exercise in this course is a prompt you type into Antigravity. You'll learn by doing, inside the very tool you're learning about. When something is confusing, you'll ask Antigravity to explain it. When you get stuck, you'll ask Antigravity to help you get unstuck.

This isn't just a clever idea — it's the most efficient way to learn. The tool becomes your tutor.

---

## What You'll Build

The course builds toward a **Habit Tracker** app — a simple web app where you can:

- Add habits you want to track ("Morning run", "Read for 20 minutes")
- Check them off each day
- See your streak
- Access it from any device via a real URL

It's small enough to finish, and complex enough to teach you everything important: frontend, database, deployment, and auth.

**Not interested in a Habit Tracker?** No problem. See [Lesson 07B](07B-choose-your-own-path.md) — you'll use Antigravity to adapt the entire course to your own app idea.

## A Note on Exercises

At the end of most lessons, you'll see a **"Try it yourself"** section with exact prompts to type into Antigravity. These are the most important part of the course. Reading is good; doing is better.

Every prompt is designed to work as-is. Copy it, paste it into Antigravity, and see what happens. Then adapt it to your situation.
